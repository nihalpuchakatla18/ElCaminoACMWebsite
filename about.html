What is ACM?

As a student chapter of the international Associtation for Computing Machinery, ACM at El Camino is the best way to advance your future in the Computer Science landscape. We welcome students of all backgrounds and skill levels to join our community and share our love for technology!

Hosting infosessions, workshops, speaker panels, hackathons, and competitions. We cover manny different topics, things such as Artificial Intelligence, VR, mobile and web development, and more. We make them Beginner friendly and Open to Everyone
